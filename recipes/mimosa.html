Mimosa

These mimosas will complete any brunch party. Don't miss out on this must-have this Sunday!

Ingredients

  • Orange Juice 750 mL
  • Brut Champagne 750 mL
  • Oranges for garnish

Steps

  1. Mix orange juice and champagne at a 1:1 ratio.
  2. Slice oranges into wheels and add a slice so that the orange may be used as garnish on the side of the glass
  3. Serve with your favorite brunch food. Cheers!
