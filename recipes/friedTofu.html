Fried Tofu

Fried tofu is both simple and delicious! These little nuggets of joy will leave you satisfied all day long. Whether you're eating them alongside meat or as a plant-based alternative, you can bet they'll complete your meal in a way that really counts.

Ingredients

  • Extra Firm Tofu 1 block
  • Cornstarch enough to coat tofu
  • Soy Sauce to taste
  • Olive or Vegetable Oil to pan-fry

Steps

  1. Press tofu in a tofu press, or cut in half and wrap in paper towels with a heavy object (such as a skillet or cutting board weighed down with cans) laid across the top of the block
  2. Once pressed, slice the block of tofu into 1-inch cubes
  3. Toss cut tofu with soy sauce first, then add cornstarch to the mixture until each piece of tofu is fully coated
  4. Pan-fry over medium heat, working in batches if necessary until the tofu reaches a golden brown
  5. Serve with dipping sauce or as a part of breakfast, lunch, or dinner!
