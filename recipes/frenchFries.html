Steak Fries/h1>

Nothing beats home-cooked comfort food, especially when that comfort food has so many less calories than what you'd get at your local fast-food chain. These steak fries are delicious, flavorful, and easy to make just in time for dinner! Serves 2-4

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes 3-4
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt 1-2 Cups for brine

Steps

  1. Wash and slice potatoes into 1/4" slices, then slice again into 1-2 inch strips
  2. Once sliced, prepare brine by adding salt to water in a bowl large enough to cover potatoes
  3. Soak potatoes at minimum 2 hours, or leave overnight. Remove from brine and pat dry with paper towel
  4. Preheat Air Fryer to 400F if machine requires, and cook at 375F for 10 minutes
  5. After 10 minutes, shake Air Fryer basket to move potatoes around before returning to the Air Fryer for an additional 10 minutes at 400F.
  6. Remove from Air Fryer and toss with salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme. Enjoy!
